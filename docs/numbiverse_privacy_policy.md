# Privacy Policy for Numbiverse

**Last Updated:** December 13, 2025

## 1. Data Collection
The Numbiverse app **does not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information from its users. All game progress, settings, and statistics are stored locally on your device and are not shared with us or any third parties.

## 2. Third-Party Access
We **do not** permit third-party advertising or analytics services within the app that could collect user data.

## 3. Children's Privacy
Numbiverse is designed for children and adheres to strict privacy standards. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. As we collect no personal data from any user, this principle is inherently upheld.

## 4. Local Storage
User preferences (like number range and audio settings) and game data (scores, unlocked stickers) are saved exclusively on your device to enhance your experience. This data is not accessible to us or any external entities.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time.
