# Privacy Policy for Kinderverse

**Last Updated**: November 16, 2025

## Introduction

Kinderverse ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable international privacy laws.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Kinderverse mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO Collect

**Local Device Storage**:
- Story progress (which stories have been read, which words have been tapped)
- App settings (dark mode, language preferences, text capitalization)
- In-app purchase records (which story packs have been unlocked)

**Important**: This information is stored ONLY on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage). It is never transmitted to our servers.

### Information We DO NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
- Location data
- Photos, videos, or camera access
- Contact lists
- Device identifiers for advertising or tracking
- Analytics or usage data sent to third parties
- Any information that could identify a child

## How We Use Information

The locally stored information is used solely to:
- Remember which stories your child has read
- Maintain app preferences across sessions
- Track which story packs have been purchased to unlock content

This information never leaves your device.

## In-App Purchases

Kinderverse offers in-app purchases for premium story packs. These purchases are:
- Processed entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play Store
- Not handled directly by us
- Subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies and payment terms

We do NOT receive or store payment information. We only verify purchase status through the app store APIs to unlock content locally on your device.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Kinderverse is designed for children and complies with COPPA. We:
- Do NOT collect personal information from children
- Do NOT use third-party analytics or advertising networks
- Do NOT allow children to share information publicly
- Do NOT use persistent identifiers for tracking

**Parental Control**: All data is stored locally on your device. Parents can delete all data by uninstalling the app.

## Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data:

**iOS**: Delete the app from your device  
**Android**: Uninstall the app or clear app data in Settings > Apps > Kinderverse > Storage > Clear Data

## Third-Party Services

Kinderverse uses the following third-party services:

1. **Apple App Store / Google Play Store**:
   - For in-app purchase processing
   - Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy
   - Required for purchase verification

2. **Expo Services** (Development framework):
   - No user data is collected or transmitted to Expo
   - Used only for app infrastructure

We do NOT use:
- Analytics services (no Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Location services
- Push notifications with tracking

## Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard secure storage provided by iOS and Android. We do not transmit data over the internet, reducing security risks.

## International Users

Kinderverse is available globally and complies with:
- **COPPA** (United States)
- **GDPR** (European Union) - Note: No data collection means GDPR compliance by default
- **PIPEDA** (Canada)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date above
- Displaying a notice in the app (for significant changes)
