## In-App Purchases

Kinderverse offers optional premium story packs.  
Purchases are:
- Processed securely by Apple or Google  
- Verified using the App Store / Play Store  
- Not handled directly by us  
- Stored only on your device

We cannot access or restore purchase data manually.  
Use **Restore Purchases** in the app if needed.
